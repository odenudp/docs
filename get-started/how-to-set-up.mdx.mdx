# How to Set Up Your Hosting Account with Xenum Hosting

Get started with Xenum Hosting (xenumhosting.com) by setting up your account. This guide provides detailed steps to ensure a smooth onboarding process.

## Prerequisites
- A valid email address for account creation.
- A payment method for purchasing a hosting plan.

## Steps to Set Up Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up for an Account">
    Navigate to [xenumhosting.com/signup](https://xenumhosting.com/signup).  
    Fill in your details:
    - **Email Address**: e.g., `you@example.com`
    - **Password**: Choose a strong password (at least 12 characters, mix of letters, numbers, and symbols).
    - **Full Name**: For account verification purposes.
    Click "Sign Up" to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Hosting Plan">
    After signing up, you’ll be redirected to the pricing page. Select a plan that suits your needs:
    - **Basic**: $5/month, 10GB SSD, 1 website.
    - **Pro**: $15/month, 50GB SSD, 5 websites.
    - **Enterprise**: $50/month, 200GB SSD, unlimited websites.
    Click "Select Plan" and complete the payment process using your preferred method (credit card, PayPal, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from `support@xenumhosting.com`.  
    Click the verification link to activate your account. If you don’t see the email, check your spam/junk folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In to Your Dashboard">
    Once verified, log in at [xenumhosting.com/login](https://xenumhosting.com/login) using your email and password.  
    You’ll be directed to the Xenum Hosting dashboard where you can manage your hosting services.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting
- **Email Not Received**: Ensure your email address is correct. Contact [support@xenumhosting.com](mailto:support@xenumhosting.com) if the issue persists.
- **Payment Issues**: Verify your payment method has sufficient funds. Try an alternative method if necessary.
- **Login Problems**: Use the "Forgot Password" link to reset your password if you can’t log in.

## Next Steps

- Learn how to use the DirectAdmin control panel in [Using DirectAdmin Control Panel](/getting-started/using-directadmin).
